## Title

Missing dynamic imports and image optimization increase bundle and runtime cost

## Description

No `next/dynamic` usage found in app source, modal-heavy components are statically imported, and raw `<img>` is used across key pages/components instead of `next/image` optimization.

## Impact

Larger JS and image payloads, slower LCP/TTI on constrained devices.

**Risk:** May require image domain allowlist and layout tuning.
