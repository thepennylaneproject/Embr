## Title

Music phase-2 vertical is exposed in production UI without a feature flag

## Description

The Music vertical is now controlled by NEXT_PUBLIC_ENABLE_MUSIC. When disabled, the app shell, onboarding discovery, composer music button/modal, post music cards, about/discovery/earnings copy, and every /music page are gated; direct /music navigation returns a 404 through getServerSideProps.

## Impact

Production builds keep the incomplete Music phase-2 vertical out of visible UI and direct routes unless the explicit feature flag is enabled.

## Suggested fix

Gate all Music routes and navigation entries behind a production-safe feature flag until the vertical is explicitly ready, or document backend completeness and update the expectation.

**Affected files:** `apps/web/src/pages/music/index.tsx`, `apps/web/src/components/onboarding/OnboardingWizard.tsx`, `apps/web/src/lib/copy.ts`
