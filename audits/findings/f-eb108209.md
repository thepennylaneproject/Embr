## Title

Music phase-2 vertical is exposed in production UI without a feature flag

## Description

The project expectations state that the Music phase-2 vertical must not be accessible or visible in production UI until backend models are complete. The web app includes public /music routes, an onboarding link to /music, and Music copy in product pages without an observed production feature flag gate.

## Impact

Production users can enter an incomplete vertical, creating broken or misleading product flows and violating a critical launch constraint.

## Suggested fix

Gate all Music routes and navigation entries behind a production-safe feature flag until the vertical is explicitly ready, or document backend completeness and update the expectation.

**Affected files:** `apps/web/src/pages/music/index.tsx`, `apps/web/src/components/onboarding/OnboardingWizard.tsx`, `apps/web/src/lib/copy.ts`
